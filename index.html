Pencils

Used different varities of B pencils.Most important pencils require based on my opinion are 2B,5B 8B. Another types of pencils you can use they are charcol pencils.charcol pencils are easy to erase compared to normal pencils but they are expensive.

Eraser

Used normal eraser's and Tombo mono eraser.Normal eraser is sufficient for makeing highlights but if your required a detailed highlights then you must have tombo mono eraser.

Blending Stumps

Used blending Stumps and differet varities of brushes to blend the shade. Unlike Mono eraser blunding stumbs are not essential, you can user your normal paper towel or cotton.which gives us the same result.

White Pens

Used for detailed highlights.White pens are used for detailed highlights, It will be handy if you have one.

Paper

Used normal A4 and chart papers. Any paper is recommended but it should not have any texture
